Searching Data

Specify 6 has two search functions for searching fields as well as related data:

  1. Simple Search enables quick searches on pre-indexed fields within Specify.

  3. Query is a more traditionally structured database search that will dredge anything out of the database with complete precision to your exacting specification.

Queries can be used to create reports in SpecifyiReport then opened in Specify and either viewed in the work space or printed.
